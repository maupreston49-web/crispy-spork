Mission Types

Every mission is designed to channel your dog’s energy into structured tasks that promote physical and mental wellbeing.

High‑Drive Ops

  • Obstacle courses and agility challenges.
  • Deep‑water decompression swims and pool drills.
  • Backpack runs and off‑trail exploration.
  • Advanced scent work and problem‑solving exercises.

Hunt & Track

  • Scent puzzles and tracking missions through varied terrain.
  • Search‑and‑find games to tap into natural instincts.
  • Wildlife trail following and trailing exercises.
  • Confidence‑building hunts that challenge the mind.

Recon Walks

  • Purposeful urban outings focusing on focus and calm.
  • Exposure to new sights, sounds, and smells.
  • Engagement games amid distractions.
  • Structured leash work to build leadership.

Gentle Patrols

  • Low‑impact hikes and sniff‑and‑stroll adventures.
  • Sensory enrichment for seniors and low‑drive dogs.
  • Calm decompression in wooded and waterfront environments.
  • Building confidence without over‑arousal.

Rescue & Adoption Programs

  • New Pack Mission: Ten missions included for dogs adopted from local rescues. Decompression and training to ease the transition into a new home.
  • Foster Support: One complimentary mission per week for foster dogs while they await adoption.
  • Guardians Missions: Specialized missions for long‑term fosters or reactive dogs needing behavioral support and decompression.
  • Custom Behavior Plans: Private, home‑away‑from‑home training for dogs needing focused behavior modification.

Facility & Service Area

  • We operate on a 100‑acre facility featuring a 10‑acre mission/training park, 20 acres of farmland, and 25 acres of wooded trails.
  • GPS tracking ensures your dog’s safety and allows you to follow along on every mission.
  • We serve Jacksonville, Ponte Vedra, St. Johns, and surrounding areas.
